PARIS

I was born and raised in Paris, I've lived there my whole life. I love this city, I think it is breath-taking.

Here are some of my favorite places in Paris.

The "Parc des Buttes Chaumont", so nice during summer.

The "Grand Rex", a beautiful environment to see a good movie.

The "Fontaine Saint-Michel", majestic and relaxing.

The cafe "La Marine", where you can see me day and night.

The "Canal Saint-Martin", my eternal break area during high school.

The amazing restaurant Kodawari Ramen, which makes you feel like you are in Japan.

The Montorgueil Street, with so much good food and restaurants.

The "Parc de Belleville", with a beautiful view of Paris, and not overcrowded like the Sacré-Coeur.

The "Comptoir général", a beautiful place to chill with your friends.

And last but not least, the Parisian Subway, so useful.
